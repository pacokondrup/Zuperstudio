About the Studio
Züperstudio
is an
artist-designer collective
dedicated to exploring the
curious tensions
between
culture and ecology
in the epoch dubbed the Anthropocene (
AN-thrə-pə-seen
).
The collective
aims to speculate on and project
alternative visions of Earthly symbiosis
.
Weaving
through the work are
three primary threads
of creative investigation:
  • - the deeply interdependent nature of our global society
  • - the present and future implications of planetary climate change
  • - the transformative power of narrative storytelling
